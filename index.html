About Me Get to Know me.
I'm a Full-stack developer based in Kwale, KENYA
Growing up I always had a keen interest in figuring how stuff works. I always wanted to scrutinise every phone or computer I got my hands on, learning all the settings and menu options. In high school I got into trouble very often because I was always in the computer room instead of the classroom. My love for code did not however materialise until later in my first year in campus. Since then, I have been involved in multiple industry projects, all the way from conception to launch. Be sure to view my portfolio.
